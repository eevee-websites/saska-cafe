What's On

Our private upstairs dining room is ideal for special occasions or a laid-back bottomless brunch. We offer flexible indoor and outdoor spaces that can be tailored for any event, including birthdays, family celebrations, corporate functions and more. With a wide selection of food - from platters and finger foods to high teas and fine dining - alongside an extensive range of wine, cocktails and coffee, Saska Cafe aims to elevate every occasion.
